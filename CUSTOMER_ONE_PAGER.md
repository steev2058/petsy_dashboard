## What is Petsy Dashboard?
**Petsy Dashboard** is an all-in-one platform for pet owners, veterinarians, clinics, marketplace sellers, and admins.

It combines:
- Pet profiles and care records
- Adoption and sponsorship journeys
- Marketplace buying/selling
- Social friends + direct chat
- Role-based professional workflows
- Admin moderation and safety tools

---

## Why people use it
- **One app, many needs:** care, community, commerce, and communication in one place
- **Trust and safety:** moderation tools, report/block flows, and role-based controls
- **Modern user experience:** mobile-first design, live notifications, clear status updates
- **Built for scale:** clear APIs, role-based architecture, and operational readiness patterns

---

## Who it serves
- **Pet Owners:** manage pets, discover adoption, sponsor pets, join community
- **Vets:** receive and complete care requests with required clinical details
- **Care Clinics:** handle clinic workflows and care timelines
- **Market Owners:** manage marketplace operations
- **Admins:** oversee users, reports, moderation, and platform health

---

## Core capabilities
1. **Pet Care Management**
   - pet records, care requests, health-related workflows

2. **Adoption & Sponsorship**
   - create, browse, and track adoption/sponsorship flows

3. **Marketplace**
   - listing lifecycle, owner actions, moderation support

4. **Notifications Center**
   - history, filters, unread badges, realtime-style refresh

5. **Social Layer**
   - friend requests, direct messaging, privacy controls, block/report

6. **Admin Control Panel**
   - user management, role governance, moderation queue, audit logs

---

## Privacy & safety highlights
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- User privacy settings for friend requests and direct messages
- Block/report tools integrated into social experience
- Admin moderation with audit trails

## Typical value outcomes

- Better user trust through transparent controls
- Higher feature adoption due to unified experience
- Lower operational friction with centralized admin tools
